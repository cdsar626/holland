CLEO Universal online shopping cart

Project Summary

Cleo is a plug-in based platform that enhances the online shopping experience. Our online shopping aggregator consolidates shopping carts from different clothing websites into one, improved shopping cart.

Challenge

Online shoppers are overwhelmed by the number of clothing websites to shop from.

Solution

The platform facilitates the online shopping experience by allowing you to compare different articles of clothing as well as manage your checkout budget. You may add items to your carts on various websites and automatically view them on the plug-in.

1. Secondary and Primary Research

Before we came up with the idea behind Cleo, we walked around Cambridge and Boston and interviewed dozens of people about their experience with the online shopping process. Additionally, we posted a survey to different Facebook groups that we are in.

2. Ideate

We sat down together as a team and discussed the pain points of our potential customers. With that in mind, we did a few brainstorming exercises. For example, we each sketched an idea on a sticky note for 1 minute and passed it to our left to add to it. We continued to do it until the sticky note circled around. We came up with an online shopping aggregator that had a compare feature and let users check out at once.

3. Test

To test our platform, we released a beta version on the Google chrome market. We observed users use it on different websites and received iterative feedback.

4. Pitch Contest

We presented our idea in front of investors and experts at a start-up incubator at MIT and our idea placed 3rd of 20 teams.
